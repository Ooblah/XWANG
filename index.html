About Me

Hi! I'm am currently a student studying Computer Science at the University of Waterloo. Growing up in a technological era, I am an avid fan of technology and always looking to explore new opportunities. I am currently working as a co-op student at IBM, working on the development of their Orion Web IDE.

I also hugely enjoy the art culture, with a history involved with fine arts and music. Having played in the Ottawa Youth Orchestra as a violinist, I have retained my love for music and currently play the violin casually, as a hobby. I am still very engaged in art however, studying a minor in fine arts. As well, starting a freelance graphic designing career, which I do both volunteer and commissioned work.

I'd like to say that my love for art has also led to my passion for fashion. I have recently acquired a kit and started to learn how to make some of my own clothes. Hopefully, I'll even approach fashion design someday...

I greatly hope to incorporate my love of art into my professional career someday, perhaps pursuing a career in a digital graphics industry, such as animation.

If you would like to learn more about me please visit the Contact section of this page!
